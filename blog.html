WHY IS IT DIFFICULT TO QUIT EATING SUGAR?

BY BIGLEE | Jul 21, 2019 | Health, Nutrition

Sugars can create a short term high and can spark up energy in the body. Sugars are at times compared to cocaine as some people enjoy the Dopamine release by brain due to sugars. However, due to the addictive nature of sugar, long-term health effects like obesity and diabetes are as a risk of sugar overindulgence. Sugar addiction can be a special risk to people with low moods, anxiety and stress.

The information provided are just an outline of what sugar craving is , its side effects and a basic plan of how you can get out of your sugar addiction.

What is omega-3 and why should you eat it?

BY JEET SELAL | Jul 2, 2019 | Nutrition

There are two types of fats that we need in our body. The non-essential fatty acids that our body has in plenty and we dont need to take it through food. And the essential fatty acids(EFA) that we need to supply only through food. They include: the omega-6 fatty acid linoleic acid (LA), and its derivatives, gamma-linolenic acid (GLA) and arachidonic acid (AA) the omega-3 fatty acid alpha-linolenic acid (ALA) and its derivatives, eicosapentaenoicacid (EPA) and docosahexaenoic acid (DHA).
